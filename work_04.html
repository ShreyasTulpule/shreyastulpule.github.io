004 : Interactive Car Sales Insights Dashboard

Link: View the interactive visualization on Tableau Public.

Overview:

To drive business success in the automotive industry by creating intuitive, data-rich dashboards that transform complex sales data into clear, actionable insights for improved strategic planning and performance.

Technology Used:

Tableau

Designed and developed a comprehensive Tableau dashboard for analyzing car sales, featuring interactive charts and filters. Visualized key metrics including total sales, cars sold, average price, top-performing models, and transmission types, with year-over-year growth comparisons. Implemented a variety of visualizations, including area charts, bar charts, donut charts, treemaps, and tables, to provide deep insights into weekly sales trends, market share, and dealer performance across regions. Enhanced user experience with interactive filters, allowing detailed exploration by gender, car details, and dealer information. Enabled data-driven decision-making and improved sales strategies through clear, actionable insights.

Outcome:

Enabled data-driven decision-making for car dealerships by providing a comprehensive, user-friendly dashboard that visualized key sales metrics, trends, and insights, leading to more informed business strategies and improved sales performance.
