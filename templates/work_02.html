002 : Interactive Sales and Customer Analysis Dashboard

Link: View the interactive visualization on Tableau Public.

Overview:

Designed and developed interactive Tableau dashboards to visualize key sales and customer KPIs, enabling data-driven decision-making and strategic planning.

Technology Used:

Tableau

Sales Dashboard: Created visualizations for metrics such as sales, profit, and quantity growth using sparkline charts, bar charts, and a map of sales by state. Integrated filters and navigation buttons for enhanced user interaction.
Customers Dashboard: Analyzed customer performance with KPIs like total customers, sales per customer, AOV, and return rates. Included top customer profiles for targeted insights.

Outcome:

Delivered dashboards that improved data accessibility, supported informed business decisions, and facilitated strategic planning through enhanced visualization.
